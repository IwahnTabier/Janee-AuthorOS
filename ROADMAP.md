# ROADMAP.md

## Vision

Build a scalable author platform for Janee Butterfield that increases visibility, grows readership, attracts literary representation, and creates opportunities for traditional publishing.

Success is measured by audience growth, industry visibility, publishing opportunities, and reader engagement.

---

# Phase 1 - Foundation

Status: In Progress

Goal:

Create a centralized Author Operating System.

Objectives:

* Organize all author assets.
* Build author database structure.
* Create opportunity tracking system.
* Create reporting framework.
* Establish content repository.

Deliverables:

* Author OS repository
* Opportunity database
* Literary agent database
* Publisher database
* Content archive
* Monthly reporting process

Success Criteria:

* All existing author assets centralized.
* Ability to generate weekly opportunity reports.
* Ability to track agents and publishers.

---

# Phase 5 - Automation

Status: Future

Goal:

Automate repetitive tasks while maintaining human oversight.

Objectives:

* Automate reporting.
* Automate content preparation.
* Automate opportunity discovery.
* Automate analytics collection.

Constraints:

* Human approval required before publication.
* Human approval required before outreach.
* Human approval required before social posting.

Success Criteria:

* Reduced manual effort.
* Increased consistency.
* No loss of quality or control.
